TERNA COLLAGE OF ENGINEERING OSMANABAD

Terna Public Charitable Trust's College of Engineering was established in1983. The college is situated at Solapur Road, Osmanabad. Terna Public Charitable Trust's College of Engineering is affiliated by Dr.B.A.T.University Lonere and Dr.B.A.M.University Auranagabad. The college is aco-education college which is well known for providing quality education to the students and is approved by AICTE and DTE. The college is known to be one of the oldest colleges in Maharashtra in the field of technical education.
